## Overview

coconutBattery Advanced is a battery health monitoring application that provides detailed information about battery condition, charge cycles, capacity degradation, and power usage for laptops and connected iOS devices. It reveals the battery data that the system does not readily expose — design capacity versus current capacity, manufacturing date, cycle count history, and temperature — enabling informed decisions about battery health and replacement timing.

Battery health display shows current maximum capacity as a percentage of original design capacity, revealing how much capacity has been lost to age and usage. The health reading provides a clear answer to the question every laptop user eventually asks: how much battery life has degraded since new.

Charge cycle tracking records the cumulative cycle count and tracks it over time, showing the usage pattern that drives battery degradation. The cycle history enables correlation between usage patterns and capacity loss, helping users understand how their habits affect battery longevity.

iOS device monitoring reads battery health data from connected iPhones and iPads, providing the same detailed battery information for mobile devices. The iOS monitoring reveals design capacity, current capacity, cycle count, and manufacturing date that iOS itself does not fully expose.

For users who want to understand and track their battery's actual condition with data beyond the simple percentage that the system provides, coconutBattery Advanced is the monitoring tool that makes battery health transparent and trackable.
